1 month of immersion in the life of Le Corbusier

From 13 April to 23 June 2024, immerse yourself in the visionary world of legendary architect Le Corbusier at the Fondation Cartier, located at 261 Bd Raspail, 75014 Paris. The exhibition offers a rare opportunity to discover his iconic works, exploring his influence on modern architecture.

Contact us

Opening times

  • Every day from 11am to 8pm, except Mondays,
  • Nocturne on Tuesdays until 10pm,
  • Rooms close at 7.45pm (9.45pm on Tuesdays).

Don't miss the chance to experience Le Corbusier's legacy and explore his lasting impact on the world of architecture. CorbuExpo promises a captivating experience for art lovers, architecture enthusiasts and anyone looking for inspiration. Book your tickets now for an immersive dive into the innovative spirit of one of the masters of modern architecture.

Prices

Discover CorbuExpo at the Fondation Cartier at affordable prices: the full price is €11, the reduced price is €7.50, and young people aged 13 to 25 benefit from a €5 discount. Take advantage of the Fondation Cartier Pass, available at €18 or €50. Admission is free for under-13s, annual pass holders, Icom card holders, press card holders, invalidity card holders, and the general public, and students. Don't miss this immersive exhibition on Le Corbusier, with prices to suit all audiences.
